# Next Design Directions

1. **Studio Console**
- A pro-audio dashboard style with mixer strips, EQ curves, and dense controls for advanced users.

2. **Minimal Mono**
- Black-and-white, typography-first interface with strong spacing and almost no decorative elements.

3. **Vinyl Lounge**
- Analog-inspired visuals with textured backgrounds, turntable motifs, and warm earthy tones.

4. **Arcade Motion**
- High-energy neon UI with animated panels, pixel accents, and gamified listening streaks.

5. **Magazine Editorial**
- Large image covers, bold headlines, asymmetric grid, and story-driven discovery blocks.

6. **Smart Car Mode**
- Big buttons, high contrast, and distraction-free controls focused on safe in-motion use.
